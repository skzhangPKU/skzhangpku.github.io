BRIEF BIOGRAPHY

Currently, Shaokun Zhang is a researcher at Huawei Device Co., Ltd. He received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Operating System Lab, Peking University in 2024, advised by Prof. Ding Li, Prof. Yao Guo, and Prof. Xiangqun Chen. His research interests include mobile app testing, privacy protection, temporal data analysis, and deep learning. He has published many papers on well-known academic conferences and journals (such as ASE, CCS, HPCA, JOS, ISSRE, TITS, ESWA, etc.). He also served as a reviewer of WWW, ISSTA, CCS, TKDE, TMC, TITS, JCST, JOS, etc. He was awarded Outstanding Reviewer of WWW 2024.
